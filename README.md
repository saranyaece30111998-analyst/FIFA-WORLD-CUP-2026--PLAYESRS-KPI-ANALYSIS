# ⚽ FIFA World Cup 2026 – Player Performance KPI Dashboard  

## 📌 Project Overview  
The **FIFA World Cup 2026 Players KPI Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of player performance, demographics, and contributions across the tournament. It consolidates critical KPIs such as player counts, average age, ratings, minutes played, and offensive contributions, while offering detailed breakdowns by team, stadium, nationality, position, and match outcomes.  

This dashboard serves as a centralized analytical tool for **coaches, analysts, and stakeholders** to evaluate player trends and performance patterns.  

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives  
- **Player Demographics Analysis** – Track total players, age distribution, and preferred foot orientation.  
- **Performance Benchmarking** – Measure ratings, minutes played, and offensive contributions.  
- **Team & Position Insights** – Compare squad balance across defenders, midfielders, forwards, and goalkeepers.  
- **Match Outcome Evaluation** – Correlate player participation with wins, losses, and draws.  
- **Stadium Impact Assessment** – Visualize goals scored by stadiums.  
- **Nationality Recognition** – Track player-of-match awards by nationality.
